Soul Type 01

The Historian

Gothic Victorian & Traditional Haveli

  • ✦ The art of depth, drama, and historical weight.
  • ✦ Craves Refuge—protected spaces and low lighting.
  • ✦ Reduces sensory input for "Deep Rest" states.
06
Historian Secret

The "Ceiling
Sink" Formula

Paint your ceiling the same color as your walls, but one shade darker.

"Instantly makes a $200,000 house look like a $2,000,000 estate."
07
Soul Type 02

The Essentialist

Scandinavian & Neoclassical

  • ✦ Visual clutter equals mental clutter.
  • ✦ Powered by Circadian Rhythm—light and shadow.
  • ✦ Symmetry creates authority and "Total Control."
08
Essentialist Secret

The "2700K"
Standard

Use 2700K (Warm White) frosted bulbs to mimic the glow of a candle.

  • • Erases harsh shadows that cause visual noise.
  • • Triggers relaxation instead of office-light stress.
  • • The color of light is more vital than the furniture.

  • ✦ Driven by Tactile Feedback—the need to touch.
  • ✦ Materials that "patina" and age beautifully.
  • ✦ Raw, honest materials that lower blood pressure.
