Find Your Home’s Soul Type

Every home falls into one dominant “Soul Type”. Most homeowners unknowingly mix styles, which creates visual confusion and lowers perceived value.

The Historian

The Earth Soul

The Essentialist

The Visionary

Soul Type 01

The Historian

Rich, deep, and full of character.

Creates a feeling of security and luxury. Perfect for homes with architectural heritage and timeless details.

Soul Type 02

The Earth Soul

Warm, natural, and grounded.

Creates a feeling of comfort and calm. Focuses on organic materials, soft textures, and a connection to nature.

Soul Type 03

The Essentialist

Clean, bright, and minimal.

Creates a feeling of clarity and space. Emphasizes light, functional design, and visual silence.

Soul Type 04

The Visionary

Bold, modern, and striking.

Creates a feeling of excitement and uniqueness. For homes that push boundaries with innovative design and bold statements.
